<div class="govuk-grid-row">
  <p class="govuk-body govuk-!-font-weight-regular govuk-!-margin-left-3">The terms of use policies explain what users can expect when creating and operating software services that use Business Gateway APIs.
  </p>
  <h3 class="govuk-heading-s govuk-!-margin-left-3">Versioning policy
  </h3>
  <p class="govuk-body govuk-!-font-weight-regular govuk-!-margin-left-3">This versioning policy outlines Business Gateway’s process for making changes to its <a class="govuk-link" href="/apis/submit-an-application-to-change-the-land-register">APIs</a>.
  </p>
  <h4 class="govuk-heading-s govuk-!-margin-left-3">What we are changing and why
  </h4>
  <p class="govuk-body govuk-!-font-weight-regular govuk-!-margin-left-3">HMLR reserves the right to introduce up to four new major versions of any API per year. When a new version of an API is released, we’ll allow six months between the deprecation and decommissioning of old versions.
  </p>
  <p class="govuk-body govuk-!-font-weight-regular govuk-!-margin-left-3">Any minor changes to existing API versions will be backwards compatible and will not require any immediate changes by our customers.
  </p>
  <p class="govuk-body govuk-!-font-weight-regular govuk-!-margin-left-3">Making these changes will mean continuing to provide a faster and more responsive service but we understand they may also disrupt the work of our customers. As a result, we’ll be clear about the changes we’re making, and when, so you can understand the risks and benefits to your own systems and ways of working.
  </p>
  <h4 class="govuk-heading-s govuk-!-margin-left-3">How we'll support you to make changes
  </h4>
  <p class="govuk-body govuk-!-font-weight-regular govuk-!-margin-left-3">Where possible, we’ll provide you with access to our sandbox test environment to run new versions of APIs before they go live. We'll also provide API schema documentation in the form of OpenAPI specifications. This should help you to understand how any changes might impact your systems.  We may also provide other resources, including:
  </p>
  <ul class="govuk-list govuk-list--bullet">
    <li>diagrams and visuals </li>
    <li>best practice examples </li>
  </ul>
 
  <h4 class="govuk-heading-s govuk-!-margin-left-3">When to expect changes
  </h4>
  <p class="govuk-body govuk-!-font-weight-regular govuk-!-margin-left-3">We know that changes will impact our customers differently and have factored this into how we communicate with you. We’ll be clear about deadlines for integrating new versions of services and provide advanced notice when services will be deprecated and decommissioned. 
  </p>
